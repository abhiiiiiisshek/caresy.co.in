SOP Protocol

What happens if something goes wrong?

Anxious families need to know we have rock-solid backup protocols. If a patient's condition worsens, our trained companions follow a strict emergency SOP:

1
Alert Family Contact

Immediate phone call and live status update to the primary emergency contact.

2
Coordinate Triage

Accompany the patient directly to the emergency triage room and assist duty nurses.

3
Operations Escalate

Central operations acts as back-office coordinator, dispatching backup care support if needed.
