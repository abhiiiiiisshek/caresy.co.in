Services

Practical help through the hospital visit.

Caresy is built for families who need a reliable human presence around appointments, tests, paperwork, and updates.

01

Hospital Companion

Presence with the patient during hospital visits, registration, movement between departments, and appointment coordination.

02

Appointment Assistance

Help with doctor details, appointment time, department navigation, queue token handling, and form submission.

03

Document Assistance

Prescription photos, reports, bills, discharge papers, invoices, and visit notes organized for family review.

04

Medicine Collection

Medicine purchase support when included, bill upload, medicine photo, and clear handover after collection.

05

Queue Management

Support at registration counters, waiting areas, billing counters, labs, and hospital desks.

Future

Pickup & Drop

Planned future service for hospital transit and full-day companion coverage.

Positioning

Caresy is not telemedicine, nursing, ambulance, or medical advice.
